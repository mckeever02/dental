Guarantee of success

Here at dental solutions, we pride ourselves on the level of service and expertise we offer our clients. A lot of effort goes into ensuring that you get the care, detail and precision we would want for ourselves. We are careful, diligent and will not be rushed. Everything we do is evidence based and as a result, we only provide treatment that is known to be effective, safe, reliable and long lasting.

Despite this, very occasionally treatments do not last as long as they should. So, for peace of mind, we guarantee our work. If a problem should occur, a like-for-like replacement will be provided free of charge.

We hope our guarantee gives you peace of mind and encourages you to protect your investment in your health and appearance.

Treatment Guaranteed for
Dental Veneers 5 Years
Dental Crowns 5 Years
Fillings 3 Years

The guarantee is dependent upon:

  • Regular six monthly check ups at dental solutions
  • Regular hygiene visits as prescribed by your dentist
  • Following recommended guidelines for aftercare
  • Using oral hygiene techniques that we advise

The guarantee does not include:

  • Any part of the work that has been completed by another dentist
  • Injury caused by accidents, sports or any third party (external trauma)
  • Unforeseen subsequent root canal treatment
  • Work which has been excluded by the dentist prior to treatment being carried out
  • NHS treatments (these have a separate guarantee)
