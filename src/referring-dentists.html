Referring
Dentists

We are delighted to accept referrals for those patients with:

  • Advanced Restorative Needs
  • Tooth Wear
  • Difficult Dentures
  • Requiring implants

We firmly believe that where possible holding on to patients natural teeth is the best option and will always endeavour to do our treatment in the most conservative manner acceptable to your patient. Please be assured that we will neither accept or approach your patient for treatment outside the remit of the referral.
